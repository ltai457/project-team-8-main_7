Team 8 – ChatGPT

We are a group of five students from the School of Science and School of Commerce at the University of Auckland. We have come together to work on a project and deliver the final outcomes to our client, Asma Shakil. Our team’s name is simple in origin. When we were filling out our team forming sheet, we struggled to come up with a name. As we considered asking ChatGPT to suggest a cool name, we thought, "Why not name our team after ChatGPT?" And so, here we are with a website designed by ChatGPT.
